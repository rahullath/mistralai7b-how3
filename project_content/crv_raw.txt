CRV token holders can earn a share of these fees by locking up their tokens, incentivizing them to support the platform and keep it running smoothly. This creates value for both traders and token holders.

Curve was created by Michael Egorov, a Russian scientist with a background in physics and cryptography. The project launched in January 2020. Curve solves the problem of high slippage and inefficient trading of stablecoins on traditional exchanges. Curve's design minimizes this slippage, making it cheaper and more reliable to trade these assets. This is important because stablecoins are a crucial part of the DeFi ecosystem.
