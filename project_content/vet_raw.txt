**1. Value Generation**

Vechain helps people lend and borrow crypto in a new way. It creates value by connecting lenders and borrowers directly, potentially offering better interest rates than traditional banks. VET token holders can participate in the platform's governance and potentially earn rewards for contributing to the network's security and stability. This creates a system where everyone benefits from a healthy lending ecosystem.

Vechain is known for its focus on supply chain management and using blockchain to track products. While it operates in the lending sector, its strength lies in its ability to verify the authenticity of assets used as collateral. This innovation helps reduce the risk of fraud and ensures transparency in lending transactions, making it a unique player in the crypto lending space.

**3. Project Size**

This makes Vechain a significant project with a strong presence in the industry.

Vechain is used globally, particularly in industries like food and beverage, luxury goods, and pharmaceuticals. Vechain was founded in 2015 by Sunny Lu, a former CIO of Louis Vuitton China. Lu brought his extensive experience in luxury goods and supply chain management to the project. The team's background in both technology and business has been crucial in driving Vechain's adoption and real-world applications.

Vechain solves the problem of trust and transparency in lending and supply chains. By using blockchain technology, it ensures that information is accurate, verifiable, and tamper-proof. Vechain's core idea is to use blockchain technology to create a transparent and efficient lending ecosystem and supply chain management system.
